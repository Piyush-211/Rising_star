About us

Rising Star English Medium School is a CBSE-affiliated institution dedicated to providing a strong educational foundation for young learners. We offer classes from LKG to Grade 4, fostering a nurturing and engaging environment where children can grow academically, socially, and emotionally. Our school focuses on a balanced approach to education, combining modern teaching methods with traditional values. With the guidance of our esteemed principal, Mr. Avinash Pawar, we aim to instill a love for learning, creativity, and critical thinking in every student. At Rising Star English Medium School, we believe that every child is a star, and with the right guidance, they can shine brightly in all aspects of life.

About Cirriculam

At Rising Star English Medium School, we follow the CBSE curriculum, designed to provide a well-rounded education that nurtures intellectual, social, and emotional growth. Our curriculum focuses on: ✅ Strong Academic Foundation – Core subjects like English, Mathematics, Science, and Social Studies are taught with a conceptual and practical approach. ✅ Activity-Based Learning – Engaging classroom activities, projects, and hands-on experiments make learning fun and interactive. ✅ Moral & Value Education – We instill ethics, discipline, and responsibility to develop well-rounded individuals. ✅ Holistic Development – Extracurricular activities, arts, music, and physical education are integrated into daily learning. ✅ Child-Centered Teaching – Our methods encourage curiosity, creativity, and critical thinking.

Rising Star English Medium School follows the CBSE curriculum, focusing on academic excellence and holistic development.
